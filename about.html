My Story

Hi there, I'm Olga, a UX/UI designer. I have taken the challenge to turn my career from quality management in the automotive industry to design.

I love simple and clear products where there is no need for explanation, and all is working right the first time to create a great experience.

My strong skills are problem solving and empathy. I can understand and see the logic behind any action or object very quickly and always propose a solution. However, I like to work based on data and perform rigorous research and test my solutions.

I prefer to work in a team than alone, and I’m not afraid to express my opinion. I like diversity and a multicultural environment.

I have Russian origin and currently, I’m based in Budapest. Thanks to my Quality Management job I had a chance to live in UK and France and work with teams from all over the world (China, US, Europe, India).

I like to travel and scuba dive, so most of the time, my holidays are around dive spots or short city breaks with my husband and two sons.
